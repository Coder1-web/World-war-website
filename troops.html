World War I Troops

  • British Expeditionary Force: The British Army's first commitment to the Western Front.
  • French Army: The main force fighting against German aggression on the Western Front.
  • German Army: The principal force of the Central Powers, known for its early successes and trench warfare tactics.

World War II Troops

  • Wehrmacht (Germany): The unified armed forces of Nazi Germany, which played a central role in the war's European Theater.
  • Red Army (USSR): The Soviet Union's army, pivotal in the Eastern Front and the eventual defeat of Nazi Germany.
  • U.S. Armed Forces: The United States military, crucial in both the Pacific and European Theaters.
  • British Armed Forces: Key players in the Battle of Britain, North Africa, and the invasion of Europe.
