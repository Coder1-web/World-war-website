Special Persons of World War I

  • Alvin York (USA): A highly decorated American soldier who became a national hero for his actions in the Meuse-Argonne Offensive.
  • Manfred von Richthofen (Germany): Known as the "Red Baron," he was the most famous flying ace of World War I.
  • T.E. Lawrence (UK): Also known as "Lawrence of Arabia," he played a key role in the Arab Revolt against the Ottoman Empire.

Special Persons of World War II

  • Anne Frank (Germany/Netherlands): A Jewish girl whose diary became one of the most poignant accounts of the Holocaust.
  • Oskar Schindler (Germany): A German industrialist who saved over 1,000 Jews during the Holocaust by employing them in his factories.
  • Audie Murphy (USA): One of the most decorated American combat soldiers of World War II, later a successful actor.
